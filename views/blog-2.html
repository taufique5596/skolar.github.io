How to crack Off-Campus Placement the next time you apply?

Off-Campus placements require additional efforts, and it can be more intimidating as compared to on-campus placement drives. But it gives you a diverse set of job opportunities, and it gives you a lot more exposure to the job market.

1. Prepare an impressive resume: The HR and Recruiting team spend a very short interval of time scrolling through your resume and hence making an impeccable resume is very important. Use only a standard resume template.

2. Check company websites for updates: most companies post their job openings on their websites so keep checking it.

3. Stay active in your network: Always stay in touch with your friends and your professors. You never know who can help you grab an opportunity.

4. Give placement tests: you can always consider giving tests like AMCAT, CoCubes, or eLitmus, as it can help you directly apply to the companies accepting their test scores.

5.Aptitude Preparation: Prepare well for your aptitude as it is the first screening test.

7. Check job portals: Register yourself on the job portals. Check the job portals regularly, and then you can apply accordingly for the required post.

8. Coding Preparation: Most of the companies also conduct a technical round along with the aptitude round so prepare well for the coding also.
