College Placements: A Definitive Preparation Guide

College placements or Campus placement is a program conducted within universities or other educational institutions to provide job opportunities to students who are in their last year or have graduated.

How to prepare for campus placement?

Before starting your campus placement preparation, make a list of your target companies, job posts you are looking for, syllabus, companies visiting your college for recruitment.

1. Start with the aptitude preparation: While preparing for the core engineering topics most of the students ignore the fact that the Aptitude test is the first screening test of the job they are applying for.
Aptitude tests generally comprise Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, and Verbal Reasoning.

2. Programming Language: even though you are not from the CS branch, they require you to have a good command over programming languages like C++, Java, or Python.

3. Core Knowledge: Every student should brush up on all the important topics of their core subjects as you can always expect a few questions from them.

4. Written and Spoken English: It’s true as in most of the interviews English is chosen as the language to communicate.

5. Resume Building: Believe it or not, the resume plays a very important role in your selection procedure. It's the easiest way to stand out from others and tell the interviewee about your skills and achievements.

6. Interview Preparation: Most of the students forget to prepare themselves for an interview while preparing them for other things. Preparing for the interviews before itself can save you from nervousness during and before the interview.
