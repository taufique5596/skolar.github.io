FAQs

How can I register for any program?

You can browse through various courses from our website and after making the payment for the course you will get the daily updates about the courses and access to our LMS (learning management system).

Can we get demo classes before registering?

Yes, the demo video lectures will be available for recorded courses.

Are all the courses recorded?

No, all the courses are not recorded, however, courses are hybrid which means you will have to attend both recorded lectures as well as live lectures. And other courses will be live.

Do I need to create an account on your website?

No, you don't need an account on our website, right after the onboarding we will give you access to our learning management system where you can start learning.

I have not received any onboarding form nor have I gotten access to the LMS.

After you make the payment, we send the onboarding form to students within 24 hours which are followed by giving access to LMS. The whole process might take up to 24-48 hours.

How can I track my progress?

You will be able to view your progress about your lectures and assessments on our LMS dashboard.

How do I communicate with the mentor?

We have a communication forum for each learning program on the LMS where you can post the question/doubts and the mentor will be available from 11:00 hrs to 20:00 hrs. You will also have weekly live doubt sessions where you can communicate with your mentor.

What if I’m not available or missed the live class?

All the live classes will be recorded and uploaded on your LMS, so you won't miss any lectures even if you were not available for the live class?

Can I download the course contents and lectures?

You cannot download the course contents or video lectures, however, all the material will be available for you on LMS and you will be given 6 months’ access.

What is the refund policy?

You can claim the refund within one week of payment for selected courses, we will refund the amount within 24 hours of the refund request.

Can I enroll for more than one course?

Yes, you can enroll for more than one course and you also will have the freedom to choose when to start the course (for live courses) by emailing us at support@skolar.in

Where can I report issues about my course?

If the issue is related to learning then you can contact your mentor on the LMS form, and other issues can be reported to the skolar support team.

What are the payment methods available at Skolar?

You can make the payment through a third party called Razorpay one of the most secure payment portals in India. You can pay through UPI, credit card, debit card, online banking, etc within Razorpay.

How do I communicate with the mentor?

Mentors will take live doubt classes along with that whatsapp assistance will also be given for every course.
