Placement training

A large portion of the students who are planning for placements have to learn quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning and verbal reasoning as major topics as these domains contain around 60 - 70 percent weightage of the test, trailed by the specialized subjects like C++, C, DSA, DBMS, OS, and networking.

Students gain proficiency with these specialized subjects while in college however in general lose handle the quant and reasoning.

Along these lines, we have built up this program which will assist students with cracking their placement exams.
