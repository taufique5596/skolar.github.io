What is the AMCAT test?
AMCAT test is one of the largest employability tests taken by a lot of candidates every year. It is a platform to showcase your employability quotient to companies hiring through AMCAT.
AMCAT test is an adaptive test as the order of questions is not fixed. It depends on whether the answer to the current question is correct or not. If the current question is answered correctly by the candidate then the next question will be a bit tougher than the current one.

What are the eligibility criteria for the AMCAT test?
• All the graduates can apply who seek jobs in the IT or non-IT sector.
• The professional can also apply.
• There is no academic criteria to appear in the AMCAT test.

Pattern of AMCAT test?
The AMCAT test pattern consists of 8 modules, in which 6 (Quantitative Ability Tech, English, Quantitative Ability, Logical Ability, Information Gathering & Synthesis, Aspiring Minds Personality Inventory (AMPI)) are compulsory and 2 modules (Automata fix, Computer literacy, Computer programming principles and applications, Electronics and semiconductors, Civil engineering, Mechanical engineering, Production engineering Marketing ,and finance) are optional. During the test, you will be given an option to select any two domain-specific modules.

What does the AMCAT exam assess?
The AMCAT Exam features question types that closely reflect the kind of thinking required at work.
English Ability to analyze and draw conclusions from discourse and reason from incomplete data,understand the meanings of words, sentences, and entire texts,understand relationships between words and concepts.
Quantitative Aptitude Measures the ability to understand, interpret and analyze quantitative information, solve problems using mathematical models, and apply the basic concepts of arithmetic, algebra and data analysis.
Logical Reasoning Measures logical thinking and decision making, including the ability to identify patterns, analyze data, and examine claims.
Personality Inventory Measures skills related to people-interaction and people-management. The test is designed based on the five-factor model or the Big 5 Personality traits to measure skills related to people-interaction and people-management.
Subject/Domain Specific Tests AMCAT also measures your knowledge of a particular subject of work. The Subject test scan helps you stand out from other applicants by emphasizing your knowledge and skill level in a specific area.

Marking rule of AMCAT ?
There is no negative marking in the test. The candidate is expected to answer all the questions in every module.

How many times can AMCAT be given ?
Candidates can take AMCAT 3 times in a calendar year only after 3 months or 90 days.
