How to prepare for eLitmus tests

eLitmus is India’s largest assessment and recruitment company. It enables companies to hire talent which performs on the job. this exam is very important for freshers as it can help students grab a better oppurtunity.

What is the eLitmus test? eLitmus pH test or Hiring Potential Test is an assessment test conducted by eLitmus to evaluate candidates’ suitability for recruitment. Now, most of the companies hire through eLitmus pH test rather than conducting their own drives. The candidates are called for interviews based upon their performance in the test. This pH test assesses the candidate's knowledge of fundamentals rather than their core subjects skills.

What are the eligibility criteria for the eLitmus test? There is no specific eligibility criteria for eLitmus. Any fresher or experienced professional can give this test. However, the companies accepting eLitmus score can have their own eligibility criteria

The pattern of the eLitmus test? eLitmus pH is an offline test. The time limit for this test is 2 hrs. This test has 3 sections. This test has 3 sections.


There is no sectional time limit. Each question consists of 10 marks.
For a good eLitmus score or percentile :
Quantitative aptitude 6- 9
Logical Reasoning 5-7
Verbal Reasoning 12-15

Marking rule of eLitmus?
The marking rule of eLitmus is unique as negative marking starts only after 25% of the attempted questions turn out to be wrong.
For e.g. If you have attempted 20 questions then the total number of wrong answers you are allowed is 5. If you get less than 5 questions wrong, then there will be no negative marking. If you get 12 wrong, then 7 of them will draw a negative mark of 50% each.
Each section is treated independently for negative marking.

How many times eLitmus tests can be given?
There is no restriction on the number of times you can take the eLitmus pH test. And there is no mandatory gap that a student is supposed to maintain before giving another pH test.
This means candidates can give the eLitmus test as many times as they want by paying the required fees.
